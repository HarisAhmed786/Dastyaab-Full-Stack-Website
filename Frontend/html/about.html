Our Mission

Digitizing Pakistan's
Service Economy

Pakistan has over 40 million skilled workers — electricians, plumbers, carpenters, painters, and more — who power the daily lives of every household and business. Yet the vast majority of them remain invisible to the digital world, entirely dependent on word of mouth to find work.

At the same time, users waste hours trying to find someone reliable. They rely on WhatsApp groups, outdated directories, and uncertain referrals. There is no transparency, no reviews, no accountability.

Dastyaab changes this. We give skilled professionals a free digital profile that makes them discoverable. We give users a fast, trustworthy platform to find and book verified experts. We make both sides of this equation faster, safer, and fairer.

"Every Pakistani household deserves access to a reliable expert. Every skilled worker deserves to be found."

— The Dastyaab Team, FAST-NUCES Islamabad, 2025
